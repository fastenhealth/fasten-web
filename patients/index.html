Fasten leverages multiple security measures to protect your data:

  • Full Database Encryption: Your data-at-rest is fully encrypted, ensuring an extra layer of protection.
  • Transport Layer Security (TLS): We employ TLS to secure the data-in-transit, guaranteeing a safe transmission between your healthcare providers and your device.
  • HIPAA Compliance: Fasten services adheres to strict HIPAA standards for comprehensive security.
